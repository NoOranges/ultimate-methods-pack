1)Find the item you want on ebay e.g earphones

2) Look in the postage section, make sure it doesn't say recorded delivery, it should say something like standard delivery, or economy delivery

3) buy the item.

4) wait for the item to arrive.

5) After it arrives, wait for about a week, then contact seller saying item hasnt arrived, they should agree to send you another one.
