Im in the sharing mood and after some research on the Barcode/Model No. Thingy;

DC143######-PCHXXYY

Replace # with Random Numbers.

Replace XX (Prefix):
11 = PSVita 3G/WiFi
10 = PSVita WiFi
Replace YY (Suffix):
01 = North America
02 = Australia & New Zealand
03 = UK/Ireland
04 = Europe
10 = South America
(More: http://www.edepot.com/playstation.html#PSP2_Serial_Number)
