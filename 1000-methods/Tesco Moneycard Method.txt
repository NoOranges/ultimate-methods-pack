First think up an excuse as to why their cafe food wasn't to your satisfaction. 

Then contact them using this link http://www.tesco.com/help/contact/

Once you've sent them an email with your excuse, you should receive an email like so:

171

Simply reply back with your chosen store & in this part you can choose an amount that you ''spent'' in the cafe. I wouldn't advise going over £30. Next you will receive an email confirming they are sending out your moneycard:

172

Enjoy.
