Step 1.) Go to http://www.thinksound.com/index.php and find a pair of headphones you want. 
Step 2.) Go to their contact us page and tell them your headphones have awful sound quality, one or both of the headphones has stopped emitting sound, etc.
Step 3.) You will receive an email asking for a proof of purchase. Either use AstroProof or make your own and email it to them.
Step 4.) Receive your new $80-$110 headphones!
